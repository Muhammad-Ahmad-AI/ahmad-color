Florescent Color 300c+

Fluorescent colors, also known as neon colors or fluorescent pigments, are extremely bright and vivid colors that appear to glow under certain lighting conditions. These colors are typically highly saturated and intense, making them stand out prominently. Fluorescent colors are commonly used in various applications such as signage, art, fashion, and safety equipment.

We Deal in vast variate of Florescent color with high temperature.

Pigment Colors

Pigment colors are colors that are derived from pigments, which are substances that impart color to materials through selective absorption and reflection of light. Pigments can be organic or inorganic compounds, and they are finely ground into powder form to be mixed with a binder or medium to create paints, inks, dyes, and other coloring materials.

Pigments are widely used in various industries including visual arts, printing, textiles, cosmetics, and plastics, among others, to add color and visual appeal to products and materials.

Titanium (Super White)

Titanium white is a popular and widely used pigment in the world of art and painting. It is a bright, opaque white pigment known for its excellent covering power and permanence. Titanium white is derived from titanium dioxide (TiO2), a naturally occurring mineral that is processed to create the pigment.

We deal in Pakistan best no1 Titanium (Super White).

Master batch Color

Masterbatch colors are concentrated pigments or additives that are used in the plastics industry to color raw polymer materials during the manufacturing process. They are essentially a mixture of pigments or dyes dispersed in a carrier resin, which is compatible with the base polymer.

Masterbatch colors are widely used in the plastics industry to color a variety of plastic products, including films, sheets, fibers, bottles, containers, and automotive parts.
